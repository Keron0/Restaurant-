ABOUT US

Welcome to Joey Rossa, where the artistry of Italian pizza meets the precision and excellence that define a Michelin star. Nestled in the heart of New York City, Joey Rossa is more than just a dining experience; it's a culinary journey that celebrates the rich traditions of Italian cuisine with a contemporary twist.

Our Story

Founded by Chef Joey Romano, Luna Rossa was born out of a passion for creating the perfect pizza. Chef Romano, a maestro of Italian gastronomy, trained under some of the finest chefs in Italy and has brought his expertise and love for authentic flavors to our kitchen. With a vision to elevate the humble pizza to new gastronomic heights, Joey Rossa opened its doors in 1903, quickly earning acclaim for its innovative approach and impeccable quality.
